Amount of decks to use-

  You can choose how many decks you wish to use in the game.  The default amount of decks to use is 2.  You can choose to use up to 5 decks in a game.  Two decks is enough cards for up to 4 people.  Sometimes it is enough for 5 or 6 people.  But to be on the safe side, if you are hosting a game with 4, 5, or 6 people, you may want to up the amount of decks to 3.  If you just want to have lots of cards, you can add more and more decks to the game.
