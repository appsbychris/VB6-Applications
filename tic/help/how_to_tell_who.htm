How to tell who's turn it is-

   It is easy to see who's turn it currently is.  Under the deck, there is a space with the label of 'Active.  Whoever is to the right of that label is the current active player.  Also, whenever someone ends their turn, in the chat screen, a message will say 'so and so has ended their turn, and now so and so's turn has begun'.  Be sure to watch for that message too.
