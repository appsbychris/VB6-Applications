Discarding a card-

   After you've drawn a card, to end your turn, you must discard a card.  To do this, just drag the card you don't want to the shape on the right-hand side of the board.  A message will pop up asking for verification.  Choose 'OK' if you want to discard that card, or choose 'Cancel' if you want to discard a different card.  If the card you are discarding is a wild card, another message will pop up, ask for verification again.  If you forgot it was wild, just press 'Cancel' and you can choose another card to discard.  But if you want to discard the wild, press 'OK'.  Once you discard the card, your turn is now over.
