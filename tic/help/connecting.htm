Connecting to a game-

   To connect to a game, you must have a server to connect to, and that server's IP address.  If you want to just play a 1 player game, check here.  Once you know the IP of the server you are wanting to connect to, go to the menu, and choose 'Connect' sub menu.  Then choose 'Connect to remote server' (Shortcut of ALT+C).  Fill in the information that is required (The IP address, and your nickname).  Choose the connect button, and the program will attempt to connect.  On the server, you will know if you are connected if your name appears in the 'Users online' section.  Another way to see if you are connected is on the bar at the top of the window will say "Tic Game Board [ver x.xx], Connected as yourname (ipyourconnectedto)".
