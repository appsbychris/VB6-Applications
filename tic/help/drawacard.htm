Drawing a card-

   Once you are connect to a game, and the game is in progress, you will need to know the basics of drawing a card.  When it is your turn, you can either draw a card from the deck, or pick up the top card on the discard pile.  To do this, simply click on whichever pile you wish to draw from.

   The discard pile is the one with the card face up.  You can only draw once per turn.
