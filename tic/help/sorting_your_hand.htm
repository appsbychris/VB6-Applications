Sorting your hand-

  To sort your hand, on the main screen, press CTRL + S.  This will bring up the sort hand window.  Arrange the cards in your hand the way you would like by selecting the card you wish to move.  Then either use the up or down arrows to move the card into the correct position.  The card in the first spot on the list will the be the first card in your hand.  When you are happy with the way your hand looks, close the sort dialog by pushing the 'X' button in the corner.  Once this closes, it will sort your hand to the way you chose.
