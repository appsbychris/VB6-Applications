How to setup for a Tic-

    Once you have a Tic, choose the 'Setup for Tic' button before you discard a card.  A new screen will come up, and your hand will be shown.  You now must set your cards aside, one group at a time.  Drag a card onto the label named 'Set for group' area, and it should appear in a list.  Once you have your first group, click the button below called 'Set Aside' the list to set it aside.  If it is a valid set, they will disappear.  Now drag your next group (if any) onto the 'Set for group' area, and continue as needed.  Once all your groups are set aside, you should have 1 card left, which that card you drag to the 'Discard this card' label area.  Once that card is in there, you can choose the 'Done' button, and you have just tic'ed out.  If you made a mistake, you can choose the 'Redo' button to redo the whole hand.  Or if you don't want to Tic out right now, or can't, choose the 'Cancel' button.
